How it works

Simple, transparent process.

Four steps. No jargon. You stay in control at every point — nothing leaves without your sign-off.

1

Discovery call

30 minutes to understand your product, your ideal customer, and exactly what a qualified meeting means for your business. No guesswork.

2

Target list build

We research and build a prospect list of verified decision-makers who genuinely match your ICP. No spray-and-pray, no low-quality data.

3

Personalised outreach

We write outreach sequences on your behalf. You review and approve every message before it sends. Your brand and voice — we do the legwork.

4

Meetings in your calendar

We manage all follow-up and scheduling. You show up to calls with context already in place — and we keep you updated throughout.

Who it's for

We're selective. That's the point.

Outbound only works when the conditions are right. We'd rather tell you it's not a fit than take your money and underdeliver.

✓
Good fit
  • ✓B2B SaaS, founder-led sales — you're closing deals yourself right now
  • ✓Some early traction — you have customers and can close when calls happen
  • ✓No outbound motion yet — it keeps falling off your plate
  • ✓Clear ideal customer — you know roughly who you're selling to
  • ✓Open to feedback — on your ICP, messaging, and positioning
✕
Not a fit
  • ✕No product-market fit yet — outbound amplifies what's working, not what isn't
  • ✕B2C or consumer products — we focus exclusively on B2B
  • ✕Need a fully hands-off service — we work closely with you, not around you
  • ✕Expecting volume guarantees — we're honest about what's realistic upfront
