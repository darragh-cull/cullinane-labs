How it works

Simple process. No black boxes.

You stay in control at every step. Nothing goes out without your sign-off.

01

Discovery call

30 minutes to understand your product, your ideal customer, and what a qualified meeting actually means for you. No assumptions.

02

Target list build

We research and build a prospect list of companies and decision-makers who match your ICP. Verified contacts, no spray-and-pray.

03

Outreach sequences

We write personalised outreach on your behalf. You review and approve every message before it goes out. Your brand, your voice.

04

Meetings in your calendar

We handle follow-ups and scheduling. You show up to calls with prospects who know what you do and are expecting to talk.

Who it's for

Built for a specific type of founder.

We're selective about who we work with — because outbound only works when the foundations are right.

✦  Good fit
  • ✓B2B SaaS, founder-led sales — you're closing deals yourself right now
  • ✓Early traction — you have customers and can close when calls happen
  • ✓No outbound motion yet — it keeps falling off your plate
  • ✓Clear target customer — you know roughly who you're selling to
  • ✓Open to feedback — on your ICP, messaging, and positioning
✕  Not a fit
  • ✕No product-market fit yet — outbound amplifies what's working, not what isn't
  • ✕B2C or consumer products — we focus exclusively on B2B
  • ✕Fully hands-off requirement — we work closely with you, not around you
  • ✕Expecting guaranteed volume — we're honest about what's realistic upfront
